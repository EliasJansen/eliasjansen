Movie posters

For a school project of a subject in Graphic Design, we were given assignments to create a photographic poster and a typographical poster.We mochten het verhaal zelf kiezen. We were allowed to choose the story ourselves. My story was about a girl who's an influencer. She went on holiday with her brother to Norway to take the most powerful pictures ever. What they didn't know is that they were chased by 2 girls who were very jealous of her. The chase ended in minor. The influencer was pushed from a fjord. On the typographic poster you see the letter 'a' as fjord and the title falling off the fjord. This illustrates the influencer being pushed from the fjord.
